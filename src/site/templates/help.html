Linear algebra

Matrices

Matrices can be initialized using the following format:
[[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6], [7, 8, 9]] becomes \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{bmatrix}\)

Vectors

Vectors can be initialized using the following format:
<1, 2, 3> becomes \(\lt1, 2, 3\gt\) , which is equivalent to \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}\)
[[1], [2], [3]] is also an acceptable format for vectors.
